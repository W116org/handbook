The information contained in the Service Manual Library is ordinarilly issued by Mercedes-Benz of North America, Inc., in conjunction with supplementary service literature and special tools supplied only to it authorised dealers. Though this library can be useful for Mercedes-Benz owners in diagnostics and troubleshooting, the repair and maintenance procedures outlined herein are intended for use by trained Mercedes-Benz service and dealership personnel.
